About

I'm Thibaut and I'm working on this project on my spare time.

I feel like I have to do something to help fight climate change, so I'm trying to use my skills to do my part.

My personal mission is to Accelerate the transition to a world without waste and hopefully this project will help some consumers to switch to buying goods without packaging.
